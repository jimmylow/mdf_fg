About Modernform Sdn. Bhd.

Modernform International markets ladies foundation wear such as brassieres, panties, girdles, bustier, camisoles, petticoats, nighties, teddies, bodies, waistbands, stockings etc. under the brand name of "Modernform".

It has to-date accumulated 25 years of exposure and experience in the undergarments counter sales industry. Creatively, combined with market knowledge, and the extremely careful selection of materials, added to the adequate use of the technology in mould development and sewing, guaranteed the highest quality in lingerie wear. At Modernform International, quality is the expression of an attitude. Our pursuit of excellence propels us to 28 years of exceptional growth. Modernform International collection provides the foundation for femininity, combining comfort and support with style and elegance.

Modernform International is devoted to meet women needs for exclusive lingerie at non-exclusive prices. As women become more sophisticated, the demand for quality and value for money increase. The women appreciate Modernform's lingerie for their exquisite styling, quality and comfortable fit. We are targeted to women who love glamorous and comfortable innerwear.
